About Me

I was born and raised in Silicon Valley and received a B.S. from San Jose State University. I spent nine years in Marketing, Sales Engineering and Management with top semiconductor companies in Silicon Valley before my love of coding put me on a new career path of Web Development.

I develop full stack applications using Javascript, Jquery, React, Node, Mongo and PostgreSQL and mobile apps using Swift and Xcode.

When I'm not developing, you can find me rooting for the Golden State Warriors, reading tech news, enjoying the outdoors or watching episodes of Silicon Valley.
